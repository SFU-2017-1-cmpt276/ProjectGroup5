Square Breathing is a proven, psychotherapy based meditative breathing exercise used to help you disrupt panic attacks, refocus your attention & regain control of your breathing.

Tap the "Start" button to begin the Square Breathing meditation exercise.

Follow the path around the square with your eyes, timing your breath to match the expansion & contraction of the circles & the vibrations of your device.

Tap the "Stats" button to view usage statistics and track your improvements as you learn to master your breathing and harness a sense of calm. You can reset your usage history at any time by tapping the "Reset" button.
