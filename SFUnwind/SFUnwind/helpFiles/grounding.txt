The 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique is a proven, psychotherapy based intervention used to disrupt a panic attack & to help you refocus your attention on your immediate surroundings.

Ground yourself in your environment, reduce mental chatter & induce a state of calm by following the on-screen guide to capture images of the world around you.

Use your device's camera to capture sequences of 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 images of things you can see, hear & touch by tapping the "Capture" button.
