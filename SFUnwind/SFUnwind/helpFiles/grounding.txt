Use the 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique to disrupt a panic attack & refocus your attention on your immediate surroundings.

Ground yourself in your environment, reduce mental chatter & induce calmness by following the on-screen prompts to capture images of the world around you. Capture a sequence of 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 images of things you see, hear & touch by tapping "Capture".
