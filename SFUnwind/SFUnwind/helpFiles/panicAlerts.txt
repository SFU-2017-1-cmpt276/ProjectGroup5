Panic Alerts are pre-written text messages that can be sent instantly with the press of a button to immediately alert and connect you with family or friends when you need support, without the stress of composing a message under duress.

Tap the "Create" button to create a new panic alert. Select a recipient, enter your message & tap save.

Send your configured alert text message by tapping the "Send" button.

Edit or delete an alert by tapping the "Edit" button next to an existing panic alert.
